Emerging EdTech Venture

  • Our Story

    TalentLink began as a university project by students from the Vietnamese-German University (VGU) who noticed a gap between academic learning and real-world work. What started as a small initiative to connect classmates with freelance projects quickly evolved into a scalable ecosystem — where education meets employment, and learning leads to opportunity.

  • Our Mission & Vision

    We envision a world where talent meets opportunity without barriers — where every person finds their path, every skill is valued, and every ambition has the chance to shine. Our mission is to empower students and businesses through intelligent, accessible, and trusted technology that helps careers grow faster and teams grow stronger.

  • What We Do

    • On-demand Projects: Post → Match → Pay by milestone.
    • Direct Hiring Pipeline: Trial projects → fast-track internships or full-time offers.
    • Skill Academy & Badges: Verified, employer-trusted learning.
    • Talent-as-a-Service (TaaS): Curated student squads for ongoing tasks.

  • Our Approach

    We combine technology, community, and credibility to make talent development accessible for everyone. Our model operates on three principles:

    • Human-first Automation: AI handles the matching — people handle the impact.
    • Education-led Growth: Each project doubles as a learning experience.
    • Trust through Verification: Skills are validated through real-world results.
